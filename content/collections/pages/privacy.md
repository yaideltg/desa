## Information We Collect

We may collect information you provide directly, including your name, email address, phone number, mailing address, vehicle interests, trade-in details, appointment requests, messages, and any information submitted when you contact us.

If you ask about financing, leasing, credit, warranties, service contracts, trade-ins, or a vehicle purchase, we may collect additional information needed to respond to your request, complete required documents, verify identity, process a transaction, or comply with legal obligations. This may include driver's license information, insurance details, employment information, income information, credit application information, and transaction records.

We also may collect website usage information automatically, such as IP address, browser type, device information, pages viewed, referring pages, dates and times of visits, and general location derived from your device or IP address.

## How We Use Information

We use information to respond to inquiries, schedule appointments, show vehicle availability, prepare quotes, process purchases, evaluate trade-ins, arrange financing when requested, provide customer service, send service or transactional messages, maintain security, improve the website, comply with law, and protect our rights and the rights of customers.

We may use contact information to send marketing messages about inventory, promotions, or services. You can opt out of marketing emails by using the unsubscribe link in those emails or by contacting us. Transactional and legal notices may still be sent when needed.

## Cookies and Similar Technologies

Our website may use cookies, analytics tools, pixels, or similar technologies to operate the site, remember preferences, understand traffic, measure campaigns, and improve the user experience. You can control cookies through your browser settings, but some site features may not work as intended if cookies are disabled.

## How We Share Information

We may share information with service providers, website hosting providers, analytics providers, payment or document processing providers, lenders, credit application platforms, vehicle history providers, warranty or service contract providers, insurers, title and registration processors, professional advisers, government agencies, law enforcement, or other parties when needed to provide requested services or comply with law.

If you request financing or leasing, we may share information with financing partners and credit-related service providers. Certain financial privacy notices or opt-out rights may be provided separately when required by applicable law.

We may also share information in connection with a business transfer, merger, sale of assets, financing, or similar transaction involving all or part of our business.

We do not sell personal information for money.

## Information Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect personal information. No website, network, or storage system can be guaranteed to be completely secure, so please use care when sending sensitive information electronically.

## Retention

We retain information for as long as reasonably needed for the purposes described in this policy, including customer service, recordkeeping, legal compliance, dispute resolution, accounting, security, and business operations.

## Your Choices

You may contact us to request access to, correction of, or deletion of personal information, subject to legal, transactional, fraud-prevention, and recordkeeping requirements. You may also ask us to stop sending marketing communications.

Some privacy rights depend on where you live and the type of information involved. We will review requests under applicable law and may need to verify your identity before responding.
